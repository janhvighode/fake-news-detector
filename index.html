AI Fake News Detection

Welcome to the AI Fake News Detection system. This project demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can help detect misinformation in online news articles.

The system analyzes the news content using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and estimates whether the news is likely to be real or fake.

Fake news spreads rapidly on social media and can influence public opinion. AI-based fake news detection systems help reduce misinformation by identifying suspicious news content and providing a trust score.
